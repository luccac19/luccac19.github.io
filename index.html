About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Florida, where my academic training brought together aspects of political science, economics, and statistics. In my day job as a political data analyst, I draw on tools from all those fields to build models and databases that help campaigns reach voters. In my spare time, I study applied problems in election administration, volunteer with pro-democracy organizations, work on contract projects, and try to get outside!
